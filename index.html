My Journey

The Spark: Engineering & Ideation

My path into product began not in a business school, but during my engineering studies at NIT Trichy, fascinated by data, technology, and participating in ideation contests. This was my first taste of how technology could be shaped to solve interesting problems.

The Foundation: Building at Scale

This curiosity led me to Citi, where I spent three years as a software developer. I moved from theory to practice, building an ML model that prevented over 100k trade failures (saving an estimated $8M+ annually) and developing data visualization tools for capital markets operations. This experience taught me how to translate complex technical solutions into tangible business impact.

The Bridge: From "How" to "Why"

While I loved the technical challenge, I was driven to get closer to the "why." This led me to an MBA at IIM Bangalore. There, I honed my strategic thinking in national competitions, winning the HUL LIME ideathon by designing a gamified marketing strategy. I learned to build the strategic bridge between code and customer.

The Accelerator: Startup Agility

While pursuing my MBA, I worked part-time at the AI startup RagaAI, juggling academic work with real-world product challenges. I learned to operate with speed and agility—conducting market research, mapping customer journeys, and collaborating on design in a fast-paced environment.

The Synthesis: Enterprise Impact

Today, as a Product Manager at JPMorgan Chase in London, I'm a key contributor to their UK retail lending products. My focus is on shaping the future of personal loans, and I have also contributed significantly to their credit card offerings, combining my technical foundation with strategic product thinking.

Mentorship & Leadership

I'm passionate about helping others grow. I currently mentor new analysts at JPMorgan Chase, and previously mentored over 20 new hires at Citi. While at Citi, I also spearheaded an internal speaker series on emerging tech, drawing audiences of over 300 colleagues.

Beyond the PRD

When I'm not deep in product specs, I'm probably planning my next trip. My passion for astrophotography was even featured on Zostel's official Instagram page (485k+ reach). It's my creative outlet for travel, content creation, and storytelling.
