My Journey

My path into product began not in a business school, but during my engineering at NIT Trichy, fascinated by data and technology and participating in ideation contests. This curiosity for how data could shape outcomes led me to Citi, where I spent three years as a software developer, building ML models and data visualization tools for capital markets operations.

While I loved the technical challenge, I was driven to get closer to the "why." I wanted to understand the user problems we were solving and the business strategy behind the code. This led me to an MBA at IIM Bangalore and a hands-on product role at the AI startup RagaAI, where I learned to operate with startup agility, conducting market research, mapping customer journeys, and collaborating on design.

Today, as a Product Manager at JPMorgan Chase in London, I'm a key contributor to their UK retail lending products. My focus is on shaping the future of personal loans, and I have also contributed significantly to their credit card offering, combining my technical foundation with strategic product thinking.
